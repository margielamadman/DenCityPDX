Mutually Beneficial

DenCity allows property owners to profit from undeveloped land while giving developers the ability maintain historic landmarks, affordable housing, and green spaces. DenCity also helps homeowners with Additional Dwelling Units to provide affordable housing to the members of their community.

Easy to Use

DenCity connects property owners with developers quickly and efficiently to facilitate the trading of Density Transfer Credits. DenCity makes it easy for homeowners to rent their Additional Dwelling Units by connecting them with potential renters.

Safe & Secure

Access to DenCity Credit Trading is restricted to verified contractors and property owners to ensure security. DenCity's ADU rental service provides comprehensive background checks and ensures homeowner safety.
